Our Mission

Cob Local connects local service providers in Coochbehar with customers who need their services. Our goal is to make it easy for workers, institutes and businesses to get customers, and for customers to find trusted help nearby within seconds.

Why Cob Local?
100% local — only providers from Coochbehar
Aadhaar verified profiles for trust
Supports providers with basic phones too
Rating and review system
Free for everyone to register or signup
